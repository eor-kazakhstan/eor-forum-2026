About the Forum
🎯
Strategic objective
Shape a national strategy for enhancing oil recovery and unlocking residual hydrocarbon reserves in Kazakhstan through 2030. Even a 1% increase in recovery factor across major mature fields could unlock tens of millions of tons of additional oil.
🤝
Who attends
All oil and gas operators, design institutes, scientific organisations, international and local service companies, global EOR experts, CCED members, industry associations, geologists and reservoir engineers.
📋
Key outcomes
Adoption of the Astana Declaration on EOR. List of priority fields. Formation of a permanent EOR working group. A pilot operations programme for 2027–2030. Proposals to the Subsoil Code to incentivise EOR implementation.
🗺
2027–2030 Roadmap
The Forum anchors a four-stage national roadmap: inventory of residual reserves, launch of EOR test pilots, introduction of legislative incentives, and full-scale digital monitoring and AI integration across assets.
